# Final Assembly

You're almost done with assembling your 3MS! Follow this guide to finish the hardware side of assembly.

.Stepn Preparing the PTFE Tubing

gcard

- stepi-175bd9bd

- Cut four equal-length segments of PTFE tubing. These should each be long enough to almost reach from the 3MS to your printer's extruder.

.gcard

.stepi-175bd9bd

.Stepn Inserting the PTFE Tubing

gcard

- stepi-c2d31929

- Insert all four segments of PTFE tubing into each of the four filament units.

.gcard

.stepi-c2d31929

.Stepn Installing the Y-splitter

gcard

- stepi-2395862d

- Insert the other end of each of the four segments of PTFE into the Y-splitter.

.gcard

.stepi-2395862d

.Stepn Tubing to the Extruder

gcard

- stepi-c4147bdb

- .gcir. Insert the remaining PTFE tubing into the other end of the Y-splitter. Cut to the proper size to reach your printer's extruder.

.gcard

.stepi-c4147bdb
